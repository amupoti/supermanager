## DEFINITION

SuperManager ACB is a simulation game that will allow you to demonstrate your ability to build the best teams using the players competing in the Liga Endesa 2024-25. Each matchday you must field a squad of 10 players, and you will earn points based on those players' statistics in each Liga Endesa matchday, which will determine your position among all participants.


## DURATION

SuperManager ACB will begin on 11 September 2024. The first scoring matchday will take place on 28–29 September, coinciding with the first matchday of the Liga Endesa 2024-25, and will run through the final matchday of the Liga Endesa 2024-25 regular season.

Team registration will remain open throughout the entire duration of the game.

The game organisation may launch new, shorter game modes during the season; the rules for those modes will be announced when they go live.


## REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS

To participate in SuperManager ACB, it is mandatory to be a registered acbID user. The organisation reserves the right to ask users to confirm their details and email address in order to continue participating.

Paid employees and collaborators of the ACB may participate outside the competition. Should they win a prize, it will pass to the next placed participant.


## GAME SYSTEM

Each participating user may create up to a maximum of 15 teams for the classic game (full season). If the organisation launches new game modes, a separate team limit will be set for each one.

Each team receives an initial allocation of €5,000,000 and must field a squad of ten players distributed as follows:

- 2 point guards ("bases" in the game)
- 4 shooting guards or small forwards ("aleros" in the game)
- 4 power forwards or centres ("pívot" in the game)

Each team may include a maximum of 2 non-EU players and a minimum of 4 locally trained (homegrown) players. The former are identified by a crossed-out European flag and the latter, for easy identification, by a Spanish flag.

Should a player's status change mid-competition — moving from Homegrown to European or foreign status, or vice versa — the organisation reserves the option to create exceptional situations.

Each team may make a total of 3 transfers per matchday, unless exceptional circumstances arise that the ACB will regulate accordingly.

The weekly transfers for each team may be made from the opening of the transfer market (after each matchday is played) until the start of the final game(s) of the next matchday that counts for the game. However, players will be locked on the market at the official kick-off time of their match if that is earlier than the transfer deadline. From that moment on, they cannot be signed or sold by any user.

It is possible to cancel a transfer provided the player sold has not yet started their match for the relevant matchday.

The system will not allow you to undo a transfer that would involve recovering or selling a player whose match is in progress or has already been played.

The game will also never allow you to undo transfers that would leave the team in an irregular situation — i.e., failing to meet the homegrown quota requirements or ending up with a negative balance.

There is a special case for postponed matches. For matches postponed before the transfer market opens for that matchday, it WILL be possible to replace the affected players without spending transfers, regardless of how many players you have.

This only applies to matches that were rescheduled in advance. You can identify the players you may substitute without affecting your limit because they will display a calendar icon next to them.

If postponements occur after the transfer market is already open, the above procedure will not apply. Instead, all affected players will be marked as injured and the transfer limit will be extended based on the number of postponed matches: +1 transfer for each match suspended for these reasons.

After a matchday with postponed matches, we will adjust the number of available transfers so you can recover the absent players. The transfer limit will depend on the number of matches postponed in the previous matchday and the number of scoring matches in the next one. We will always communicate these situations through the game, acb.com, and the X profile @SuperManagerACB or Instagram @supermanageracb.


## SCORING

Each player will receive a score for every matchday they play. This score equals their ACB valuation for that match, with a 20% bonus if their team wins; this bonus does not apply if the player's valuation is zero or below. The ACB valuation is calculated as follows:

**VAL = (PTS + AST + REB + BLK + STL + FD + FTM + 2PM + 3PM − TO − BLKA − PF − FTA − 2PA − 3PA)**

- PTS = Points
- AST = Assists
- REB = Rebounds
- BLK = Blocks in favour
- STL = Steals
- FD = Fouls drawn
- FTM = Free throws made
- 2PM = 2-point field goals made
- 3PM = 3-point field goals made
- TO = Turnovers
- BLKA = Blocks against
- PF = Personal fouls
- FTA = Free throws attempted
- 2PA = 2-point field goals attempted
- 3PA = 3-point field goals attempted

If a player does not participate in a match they receive 0 points. A team's score is obtained by adding up the points of all 10 players (including negatives and those who received the 20% victory bonus). Each team has a weekly score as well as a cumulative total across all matchdays, which is used to build the overall/final standings.


## PLAYER PRICES. BUYING AND SELLING.

Players' market value will evolve throughout the matchdays based on their statistics. Their price depends on their average ACB valuation including the 20% victory bonus, at a rate of €50,000 per point.

Users may access the market during the window between matchdays, when the market is open, to sell or sign any player in their squad at the price set by their current valuation. A player's price will change each matchday, but the price will never fluctuate by more than 15% from one matchday to the next, even if a larger change would otherwise be warranted.

Buying and selling players provides an opportunity to increase the money initially available to the team and thereby pursue higher-value signings.


## STANDINGS

The SuperManager ACB.COM game organisation will compile and publish the following standings:

- Best score by matchday
- Best score in the Overall Standings
- Best Brokerbasket index (total team valuation plus cash balance)

**Note:** Teams competing for the Brokerbasket must participate in 90% of matchdays to appear in this category's standings. This means having a valid team (confirmed, with 10 players and quota rules met) in 30 of the 34 game matchdays. If this participation threshold is not met, the team will no longer appear in the Brokerbasket standings, although it continues to compete without issue in the other game standings.


## PRIVATE LEAGUES

In addition to participating in the game's overall standings, each user may create one or more private leagues to challenge friends, colleagues, or acquaintances and find out who the best SuperManager is.

To create a private league, go to Standings > My Leagues > Create or join a League. When you create your league, you will be given a code. You must share this access code with the users you want to participate in your league.


## CLUB LEAGUES

In addition to any leagues a user chooses to join, each user will automatically become part of the Official League of the Liga Endesa club they marked as their favourite during the game's registration process.

It is possible to change your favourite club at any time from the user area, but you can only compete for prizes in the official club league to which you belong at the time the deadline closes (26 October at 18:00, the start date of the fifth Liga Endesa matchday).


## INCOMPLETE TEAMS

If a team enters a matchday (after the transfer window closes) with fewer than the required 10 players, whether by mistake or intentionally, its score for that matchday will be reduced by half (50%). Players in the team are understood to be those who are signed, regardless of whether they are injured or otherwise out of competition.


## TIEBREAKERS

If two teams are tied in the Standings for a given matchday, the tie will be broken using the following criteria, in the order listed:

1. Higher team monetary value
2. Better score in the Overall Standings
3. Draw (random)

If two teams are tied in the Brokerbasket Standings, the tie will be broken using the following criteria, in the order listed:

1. Better score in the Overall Standings
2. Draw (random)

If two teams are tied in the Overall Standings, the tie will be broken using the following criteria, in the order listed:

1. Better position in the Brokerbasket Standings
2. Draw (random)


## MATCH POSTPONEMENTS

If a scheduled postponement of a match occurs (meaning it is known before the transfer market for the current SuperManager matchday opens) and it is played outside the normal matchday window, the organisation will discard the points from that match for SuperManager ACB and will allow the players from both teams involved to be substituted without counting against the weekly transfer limit. These players will be shown in the market with a red Calendar icon with a red cross in the centre.

If the postponement occurs after the SuperManager matchday transfer market is already open, the organisation will first consider allowing the SuperManager matchday to proceed normally, extending its end date until the postponed match is played. However, if circumstances arise that create doubts or issues with that initial approach, the organisation reserves the right to act unilaterally and exclude the match from the computation, announcing its decision through all channels. In this case, the weekly transfer limit will be extended by 1 for each postponed match.

Notwithstanding the above, the organisation reserves the right to additionally regulate the weekly transfer limit based on special circumstances, such as an accumulation of postponements or the return of players who had left the market.

- 6th–10th place prizes: Official SuperManager ACB merchandise pack (valued at €40).

**Brokerbasket ACB 2024-25:** The winner is the team that finishes the game in the best financial position, defined as the sum of the total valuation of the 10 players plus the team's cash balance.
- 1st place prize: A double season ticket for the Liga Endesa 2025-26 (mid-category). Valued at €400.
- 2nd and 3rd place prizes: Official SuperManager ACB merchandise pack (valued at €40).

- 2nd and 3rd place in the Premium Private League (Liga Luisheel): Official SuperManager ACB merchandise pack (valued at €40).

## PRIZE DELIVERY RULES

Winners will be announced on ACB.COM and the ACB's social media channels. They will be contacted by email or phone within 10 days from the day following the relevant matchday. Users are strongly encouraged to keep their registration details up to date for easier and more efficient communication.

The winner must respond to this communication within 15 days of the date the notification email was sent. After those 15 days the prize will be considered forfeited and will pass to the next placed participant.

If the winner does not have a valid email address or phone number, or if the notification message is rejected by the winner's server, the game organisation reserves the right to consider the prize forfeited and pass it to the next placed participant.

All prizes will be sent by post to the address provided by the winner. The game organisation accepts no responsibility for possible losses or negligence in prize delivery that could affect its integrity.

The prize includes shipping within Spain and Andorra. If the winning participant resides outside these territories, the costs required to receive the prize will be borne by the winner, who must confirm acceptance of the prize within 48 hours of the communication ACB sends to notify them of the contest result. If the winner decides to bear the shipping costs, they must coordinate with ACB for collection by the courier service contracted by the winner.
